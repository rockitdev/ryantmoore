## The problem

Small heating and cooling companies lose money in the small gaps: a call missed while everyone is out on a job, a quote nobody follows up on, an appointment no one confirms. The owners know it is happening. They just do not have a spare person to sit by the phone, and the big software built for this is far too much for a handful of trucks.

## What I built

A set of tools that quietly fill those gaps — without hiring anyone new.

- **Always picks up** — calls and texts get answered, sorted, and replied to around the clock.
- **Books and reminds on its own** — appointments get set, confirmations go out, and follow-ups happen without anyone remembering to do them.
- **Reads the message for you** — AI takes a rambling voicemail or email and turns it into a clear, tidy note the rest of the system can act on.

The rule behind all of it: **it has to run on its own.** No dashboard to babysit, no daily fiddling. If a person has to remember to press a button, it has already failed.

## The result

Calls get answered, jobs get booked, and customers hear back — the kind of steady, reliable service a small shop usually can't afford to staff for.

This system is now a productized service for heating and cooling companies at [rockitdevelopment.com](https://www.rockitdevelopment.com).
