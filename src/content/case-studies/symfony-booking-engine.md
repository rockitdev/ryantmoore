## The problem

A booking engine is the one page a travel business cannot afford to get wrong. It is where a casual browser turns into a paying customer — and where a slow step, a stale price, or a confusing form quietly costs the sale. It has to show the right fares and the right availability every time, take payment cleanly, and never sell the same spot twice — all while feeling effortless to the traveler.

## What I built

I built a Symfony booking engine for a travel & transport client — the full path from searching for a trip to a confirmed, paid booking.

- **Right fares, every time** — live pricing and availability pulled straight from the booking system, so what the traveler saw was always what they got.
- **Search to payment in one flow** — a clean, guided path from picking a trip to paying for it, with no dead ends or surprises.
- **Correct under pressure** — the unglamorous logic that keeps a booking engine honest: holding spots, handling payment, and never double-selling a seat.

## The result

The booking engine launched and did the part that matters to the business: a booking flow travelers actually finished, and a system the team could trust with real money — holding spots, taking payment, and never double-selling a seat.
