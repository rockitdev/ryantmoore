## The problem

A booking engine is the one page a travel business cannot afford to get wrong. It is where a browser turns into a paying customer. A slow step, a stale price, or a confusing form quietly costs the sale. It has to show the right fares and open seats every time. It has to take payment cleanly and never sell the same spot twice. And it has to feel easy the whole way.

## What I built

I built a Symfony booking engine for a travel and transport client. It runs the full path: search for a trip, then book and pay.

- **Right fares, every time** — live prices and open seats pulled straight from the booking system. What the traveler saw was what they got.
- **Search to payment in one flow** — a clean, guided path from picking a trip to paying for it. No dead ends, no surprises.
- **Correct under pressure** — the dull logic that keeps a booking engine honest. It holds spots, handles payment, and never double-sells a seat.

## The result

The booking engine launched and did what matters to the business. Travelers actually finished the booking flow. The team could trust it with real money. It held spots, took payment, and never sold a seat twice.
